# Project Overiew
An end-to-end sales analysis project built on **9,994 orders** using **Google Sheets**, uncovering revenue trends, regional performance, and customer insights through an interactive dashboard.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

To analyze **4 years of retail sales data (2014–2017)** and identify key patterns in revenue, profitability, regional performance, and customer behavior, presented as an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend

**Chart Type:** Line Chart

**Purpose:** Visualizes seasonal patterns and sales performance across 48 months (2014–2017).

### 2. Sales by Category

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

**Purpose:** Compares revenue performance across Furniture, Technology, and Office Supplies.

### 3. Sales by Region

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

**Purpose:** Shows revenue distribution across Central, East, South, and West regions.

### 4. Top 10 Customers

**Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart

**Purpose:** Highlights the highest revenue-generating customers and their contribution to overall sales.

### 5. KPI Summary

**Chart Type:** Metric Cards

**Metrics Included:**

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin (%)
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Total Orders

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
* The West region contributed significantly to overall sales.
* A small group of customers generated a large share of total revenue.
* Sales exhibited recurring seasonal patterns over the four-year period.
