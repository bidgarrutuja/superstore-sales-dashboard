** Superstore Sales Dashboard **
  An end-to-end sales analysis project built on 9,994 orders using Google
  Sheets — uncovering revenue trends, regional performance, and customer
  insights through an interactive dashboard.
  
📌 Project Objective
  To analyze 4 years of retail sales data (2014–2017) and identify key
  patterns in revenue, profitability, regional performance, and customer 
  behaviour — presented as an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

📁 Dataset
  DetailInfoSourceKaggle — Sample Superstore Dataset
  Rows                     9,994 orders
  Columns                  21 fields
  Period                   January 2014 – December 2017

🛠️ Tools Used
    Tool                   Purpose
    Google Sheets          Data cleaning, Pivot Tables, Charts, Dashboard
    Pivot Tables           Aggregating sales, profit, and order data
    Pivot Charts           Line, bar, and horizontal bar visualizations
    Slicers / Filters      Interactive filtering by Category and Region

📊 Dashboard Components

1. Monthly Sales Trend
Chart Type: Line Chart
Purpose: Visualizes seasonal patterns and sales performance across 48 months (2014–2017).

2. Sales by Category
Chart Type: Bar Chart
Purpose: Compares revenue performance across major product categories: Furniture, Technology, and Office Supplies.

3. Sales by Region
Chart Type: Bar Chart
Purpose: Shows revenue distribution across the four regions: Central, East, South, and West.

4. Top 10 Customers
Chart Type: Horizontal Bar Chart
Purpose: Highlights the highest revenue-generating customers and their contribution to overall sales.

5. KPI Summary
Chart Type: Metric Cards
Purpose: Displays key business metrics including:
• Total Revenue
• Total Profit
• Profit Margin (%)
• Average Order Value (AOV)
• Total Orders
